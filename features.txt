Side scroller
Auto move right
Actions:
  Jump / Fast Fall [A]:
    If player is grounded:
      Sets player horizontal speed to 0
    Else, if player is midair and has a double jump:
      Gains small vertical boost while maintaining momentum
      If jumped immediately after hitting an enemy with a dash, jumps forward with dash speed
      Else, jumps forward with regular horizontal speed
    The player's double jump is refreshed upon touching the ground, and can only be used once until refreshed again
    Otherwise, set vertical speed to maximum downwards and acts as an attack
    If a player hits an enemy while fast falling, the enemy is destroyed and the player receives a small vertical boost
  Dash [X]:
    If the player is grounded or has air dash
    Increases player horizontal speed
    Sets player vertical speed to 0
    If a player connects with an enemy mid-dash, the enemy is destroyed and both the dash and the double jump are refreshed
